Opportunities: AI and Robotics, the Future of Industry and the Military

Robot soldiers are a fast approaching future, and it is our job to be prepared for it. Since World War II, there have been many remote-controlled robots used in the military. However, AI is becoming more advanced over time, leading to complex military innovations with destructive capabilities. "The coming generation of 'warbots' will be immensely more sophisticated, and their development raises troubling questions about how and when we wage war."

The importance of 'Robot Soldiers' or military AI lies in the future of warfare and the potential to reduce many issues caused by war. For example, the average cost of maintaining a soldier per year is $136,000, while a robot soldier like the MIS UGV can cost between $10,000 and $200,000 to produce, "with low maintenance costs in mind." This significant difference in costs will greatly reduce the need for military expenditure. Additionally, robots do not require training, unlike their human counterparts, making them battle-ready as soon as they are produced, resulting in improved availability and cost-effectiveness.

The importance of robot soldiers to the military extends beyond cost reduction. They play a crucial role in limiting casualties on the battlefield by replacing humans. By reducing human involvement in war, we can avoid human fatalities. If robot soldiers were to replace humans, we would witness a massive increase in industrial production and advancements in other fields, thanks to the reduction of deaths caused by war. This highlights the significance of military robots and how their deployment can help decrease the cost of war and its toll on people.

Increasing the number of robot soldiers on the field will enhance the safety of human soldiers. With the MIS UGV, we have reliable transportation and improved maneuverability. Robot soldiers are capable of assisting their human counterparts more effectively, as they lack emotions and self-preservation instincts that may hinder mission quality. Moreover, in challenging situations, robot soldiers can carry out their missions with speed and accuracy. By overcoming physical limitations, they offer numerous opportunities in war, surpassing human capabilities and withstanding harsh conditions, ultimately reshaping mission execution with precision and effectiveness.

The introduction of AI generates opportunities for advancements within the military in various fields, including medicine, engineering, disaster relief, and training. With today's technology, robots can undertake dangerous jobs that would otherwise put human specialists at risk. Not only do they reduce danger, but they may also be more effective in saving lives compared to their human counterparts. The opportunities generated by robots in the military have the potential to bring extensive benefits to all fields, decreasing danger, increasing the likelihood of success, and lowering casualty rates.
